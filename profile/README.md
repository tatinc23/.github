# TAT Inc

TAT Inc is a Portland-based AI product studio building a connected ecosystem of voice agents, automation tools, and SaaS platforms — from Bebop (a live AI voice cofounder) to TATclaw (the command center dashboard), Shabba (restaurant AI SaaS), and a growing suite of MCP-powered packages. Every product is wired together through shared auth, payments, logging, and config layers, letting one person move at the speed of a team.
